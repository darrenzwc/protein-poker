The Circos-style plot shows a number of layers, starting from the outer layer representing color and size distinguished chromsomes, moving clockwise from chromosome 1 to 22, then X and Y. Inside the chromosomes ring, you can see a line representation of copy number mutations. When expanded (by clicking a chromosome), more details on the precise copy number at specific locations is revealed. Hover the bars for further details.

Moving inwards, counts of transcripts are shown as scatter plots. The more outer the point, the more transcripts affected by the mutation at that coordinate. The points are both colorand shape coded, green and blue points showing the insertions and deletions, whereas red points show single point mutations. Those SNPs marked by a cross indicate mutations with an associated ClinVar record.

Genome of for protein

Clicking different colored areas of the Genome will indicate shared expressions that are repeated.

Here's a breakdown of the data:

‍Gene: Genes are represented by symbols such as SMARCA4, TP53, and KRAS. Each line represents a different gene.
‍
‍Chromosome (Chr): Indicates the chromosome where the genetic variation occurs (e.g., Chromosome 19, Chromosome 17, Chromosome 12).
‍
‍Start and End Positions (From, To): These are specific locations on the chromosome where the genetic variation is found. The 'From' and 'To' columns show the range of positions for the variation.
‍
‍Consequence: Describes the impact or type of variation observed in the gene. Variants are classified based on their effects, such as missense_variant (a change in one DNA base pair leading to a different amino acid in the protein), stop_gained (an alteration causing a premature stop codon), exon_variant (a variant occurring within an exon of a gene), downstream_gene_variant (a variant occurring in the downstream region of a gene), etc.

‍Count: Indicates the number of occurrences or individuals within the cohort exhibiting the specific genetic variation listed for that gene.

Example of reading the table: SMARCA4 gene located on Chromosome 19 has variations at position 11144847 with a C to T change. There are 18 occurrences of a missense_variant and 8 occurrences of an exon_variant at this position within this cohort.
